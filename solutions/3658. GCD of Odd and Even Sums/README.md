# 3658. GCD of Odd and Even Sums

## Easy

You are given a positive integer `n`.

Calculate the greatest common divisor (GCD) of two computed values:
- **sumOdd**: sum of the smallest `n` positive odd numbers
- **sumEven**: sum of the smallest `n` positive even numbers

**Constraints:**
- `1 ≤ n ≤ 1000`

**Follow-up:** Can you derive a mathematical formula to solve this in O(1) time?
