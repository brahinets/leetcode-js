# 2144. Minimum Cost of Buying Candies With Discount

## Easy

A store is selling candies at a discount. For every two candies you buy, the store gives you a third candy for free.
The free candy has a price equal to or less than the minimum cost of the two candies you bought. You are given a
0-indexed integer array `cost`, where `cost[i]` is the cost of the `i`th candy. Return the minimum cost of buying
all `cost.length` candies.

### Constraints:

- `1 <= cost.length <= 100`
- `1 <= cost[i] <= 100`
